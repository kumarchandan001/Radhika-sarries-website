Drape yourself in royal elegance with our Deep Maroon Kanjivaram Bridal Silk Saree. Handwoven in Surat with the finest silk threads, this masterpiece features traditional temple borders and intricate floral motifs rendered in pure gold zari. The rich, vibrant maroon color symbolizes auspicious beginnings, making it the perfect choice for South Indian weddings and grand festive occasions.

The saree comes with a matching unstitched blouse piece that can be tailored to your unique style. Its lustrous texture and heavy drape promise to make you the center of attention while offering incredible comfort throughout long ceremonies.

  • Composition: 100% Pure Kanjivaram Silk with Tested Zari.
  • Weave: Handloom woven by master artisans in Surat.
  • Care Instructions: Dry clean only. Do not bleach. Keep folded in a cool, dry place. Avoid spraying perfume directly on the zari work.
  • Ironing: Iron on the reverse side using low heat.
